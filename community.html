Woodland Hills Government

**The Woodland Hills government leaders hold meetings on the fourth Tuesday of every month. All interested may attend the meeting to listen or bring issues, concerns, or announcements.**
Please see below for information on our leaders, and how to contact them.

Mayor Tilford has been serving in the Woodland Hills Government for over 15 years, beginning as a commissioner. Under his leadership, we have experienced improvements in responsiveness to residents and their concerns, as well as several major projects in Hardy Brinly Memorial Park. He serves with his wife of over 40 years, Sally Tilford, and plans to continue to serve in some capacity for the forseeable future.

Mike serves as a commissioner for the City of Woodland Hills, and is currently in his fourth term. Mr. Ochs is still active as a Seargent Major in the United States Army, and brings a unique level of leadership to the Board of Commissioners.

Sally Tilford serves as a commisioner for the City of Woodland Hills. She has served with her husband, David Tilford for over a decade, and her enthusiasm is never more evident than it is during the Woodland Hills Summerfest, where she cooks for hundreds in the heat every year.

Brad Ricca currently serves as a commisioner for the City of Woodland Hills. His spirit for service is easy to see, especially in volunteering around the neighborhood during pickup events, along with his wife and children.

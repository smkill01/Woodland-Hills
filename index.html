Welcome to the City of Woodland Hills

The city of Woodland Hills, a neighbor of the city of Middletown, is a home-rule class city located in the gently rolling hills of eastern Jefferson County, Kentucky. Founded in 1962 as a new single-family development in the suburban Louisville area it provides a beautiful and safe environment for all of its over 700 residents. The city amenities include the 7-acre Hardy Brinly Park which provides ample green space for all sorts of outdoor activities, including a newly-completed running path.

Woodland Hills is conveniently located within walking distance of the Eastern High School and Hite Elementary School campus. The interchange of I-64 and I-264 is within 2 miles of the city giving direct access to Louisville and outlying communities.

Notice

The Woodland Hills Board of Commisioners has approved criteria for sidewalk work. Residents with "Trip-Hazards" (raised sections of sidewalk measuring 5/8" or higher) are encouraged to report to Board members. Safety for all residents and visitors is a top priority!
